How It Works

1

Upload Your Legal Document

Upload any legal file — NDA, contract, agreement, MoU, employment terms. The system instantly identifies what type of legal document it is.

2

Get Instant, Clear Understanding

The AI summarizes the document in simple, everyday language. No jargon. No confusion. No legal background needed.

3

Ask Questions Like You're Chatting

Need clarity on a clause? Ask the AI. It will explain obligations, risks, confidentiality terms, and more — all in human language.

Why This Project Matters

This solution was built for the DevCareer × Raenest Freelancer Hackathon: Democratizing Legal Literacy with AI.

The mission is deeply personal. Someone close to the founder signed an NDA without fully understanding it — and it led to unexpected legal trouble.

Many people sign contracts, NDAs, and agreements without grasping what they mean. This tool empowers individuals to make informed decisions — reducing legal risks dramatically.

Powered by AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), LegalEase AI bridges the knowledge gap and helps everyday people protect themselves.
